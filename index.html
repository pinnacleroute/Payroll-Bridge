How Gross Pay is Calculated
  • Regular Pay = Hourly Rate × Regular Hours
  • Overtime Pay = Hourly Rate × 1.5 × Overtime Hours
  • Mileage Pay = Miles Driven × Per-Mile Rate
  • Gross Pay = Regular + Overtime + Mileage + Bonus/Reimbursements

What does "API Integration" mean in plain English?

Think of the API as a secure pipeline between your GPS tracking system and the payroll engine. Your GPS system sends the approved driver data through this pipeline, and the payroll engine responds back with completed calculations, tax figures, and payment confirmations — all automatically, in seconds.

How This Works — In Simple Terms
A plain-language walkthrough of the entire GPS-to-payroll process
01
🛰️
GPS Tracks the Work
Your GPS/admin system automatically records each driver's working hours, miles traveled, and trips completed — every shift, every day.
02
✅
Admin Reviews & Approves
Your admin team reviews the GPS data, checks for any issues, and marks each driver's records as approved before they move to payroll.
03
🔄
Data Flows to Payroll
Approved work data is automatically sent to the payroll system via a secure API connection — no manual re-entry needed.
04
💵
Payroll is Calculated
The system calculates each employee's earnings based on their hourly rate, overtime, miles driven, and any bonuses or reimbursements.
05
📊
Taxes are Computed
A built-in tax engine automatically calculates all applicable federal, state, and local taxes — including Social Security and Medicare — for every employee.
06
🏦
Employees Get Paid
Employees receive their take-home pay through direct bank deposit (ACH) or a paper check — whichever method they have set up in their profile.
07
📤
Taxes Filed Electronically
The system prepares and files all payroll tax documents electronically to the IRS and relevant state agencies — on time, every time.
08
📁
Reports & Compliance Stored
Every payroll action is logged in an audit trail. Complete reports are available for your records, your accountant, or any regulatory review.
